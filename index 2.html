artist bio

nicksidar aka. NICK SIDARCHUK is a multidisciplinary artist from Kildare (IE) who studies Fine Art Media in the National College of Art and Design in Dublin. nicksidar is currently based in Arnhem (NL) studying Design Art Technology in ArtEZ University of the Arts. nicksidar's work is in a constant dialogue with contemporary culture and new technologies. their work explores ranges of mediums and forms, constantly evolving and transmutating with time.
